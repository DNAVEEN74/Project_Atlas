**Last Updated: February 18, 2026**

PrepLeague ("we," "us," or "our") operates prepleague.com (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our SSC CGL exam preparation platform.

By using PrepLeague, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted and hashed)
- Exam preparation details (target exam date, preferred subjects)

**Payment Information:**
- Payment method details (processed securely through Razorpay)
- Billing address
- Transaction history

**Practice Data:**
- Questions attempted and answers submitted
- Time spent per question
- Session history (practice sessions and sprint mode)

**Optional Information:**

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited and features used
- Practice patterns and frequency
- Device type, browser type, and operating system
- IP address and general location (city/region level)
- Session duration and timestamps

**Performance Data:**
- Accuracy by topic and difficulty level
- Speed analytics (time per question)
- Streak information
- Progress over time

**Cookies and Similar Technologies:**
- Authentication cookies (to keep you logged in)
- Preference cookies (to remember your settings)
- Analytics cookies (to understand how you use the platform)

We use your information to:

**Provide Core Services:**
- Create and manage your account

- Track your progress and weak topics
- Generate analytics and insights
- Provide sprint mode with timed practice sessions

**Improve Your Experience:**
- Recommend questions based on your weak areas
- Adjust daily targets based on your exam date
- Show speed benchmarks compared to other users
- Identify patterns in your mistakes

**Platform Operations:**
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Send important account notifications (password resets, subscription updates)
- Respond to your support requests
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Maintain platform security

**Communication:**
- Send practice reminders (if you enable them)
- Notify you of new features or content
- Send promotional offers (you can opt out anytime)

**Analytics and Research:**
- Understand how users interact with the platform
- Improve our question database and difficulty ratings
- Develop new features based on usage patterns
- Generate anonymized statistics (we never share individual user data)

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, you have the right to:

**Access Your Data:**
- Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- View your complete practice history and analytics

**Correct Your Data:**
- Update your profile information anytime
- Request correction of inaccurate data

**Delete Your Data:**
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Request removal of specific information

**Data Portability:**
- Export your practice history as CSV
- Download your performance reports

**Opt-Out of Marketing:**
- Unsubscribe from promotional emails (link in every email)
- Disable practice reminders in settings

**Withdraw Consent:**
- Disable optional data collection (like analytics cookies)
- Refuse optional features that require data sharing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **privacy@prepleague.com**

We will respond within 30 days.

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Passwords hashed using bcrypt
- Database access restricted and encrypted at rest
- Regular security audits and updates

**Organizational Measures:**
- Access to personal data limited to authorized personnel only
- Regular staff training on data protection
- Incident response procedures in place

**Payment Security:**
- We never store full credit card details
- All payments processed through PCI DSS compliant Razorpay
- Tokenized payment methods for recurring subscriptions

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Children's Privacy

PrepLeague is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

If you are under 16, please do not create an account or provide any personal information. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us at privacy@prepleague.com.

## 8. Cookies Policy

We use the following types of cookies:

**Essential Cookies (Required):**
- Authentication - keeps you logged in
- Security - prevents fraud and unauthorized access

**Functional Cookies (Optional):**
- Remember your subject preferences (Quant/Reasoning)
- Save your filter settings
- Store UI preferences (dark mode, font size)

**Analytics Cookies (Optional):**
- Google Analytics - understand how users navigate the platform
- Internal analytics - track feature usage and performance

You can disable optional cookies in your browser settings. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they're required for the platform to function.
